Data Analysis

Exploring the travel times on the MBTA helps us understand the efficiency of our public transit system.

Long wait times can significantly affect commuters' daily lives, highlighting the need for continuous improvements.

The data also reveals patterns that can inform future transit developments and initiatives.

Ultimately, effective communication about travel times and service updates is essential for public confidence in the system.
